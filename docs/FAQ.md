# Cadia FAQ

Frequently asked questions about Cadia, the Cadia Dashboard, and Cadia’s Discord bot features.

## Is Cadia owned by Discord?

No. Cadia is an independent third-party Discord bot. Cadia is not owned, operated, endorsed, or sponsored by Discord.

## What data does Cadia store?

Cadia only stores data needed to operate its features.

This may include Discord IDs, server settings, command usage, RPG progress, moderation records, ticket data, dashboard settings, and other feature-related configuration.

## Can I delete my data?

Yes. You can request data deletion by contacting Cadia support through the [Support Server](https://discord.gg/26R7kXa6dx) or by emailing [business.malikjohn@gmail.com](mailto:business.malikjohn@gmail.com).

Some data may be kept temporarily when needed for security, backups, abuse prevention, legal reasons, or active investigations.

## Are Cadia RPG items worth real money?

No. Cadia RPG items, currency, gear, rewards, ranks, levels, and progress are virtual only.

They have no real-world cash value and cannot be exchanged for real money.

## Can Cadia ban or blacklist users or servers?

Yes. Cadia developers may restrict, block, blacklist, or remove access from users or servers that abuse Cadia.

This may include exploiting bugs, breaking rules, spamming, attacking the service, bypassing moderation, or using Cadia in a harmful way.

## Is Cadia guaranteed to stay online?

No. Cadia is provided as-is.

We try to keep Cadia stable and available, but downtime, bugs, resets, maintenance, data issues, or feature changes may happen.

## Who is responsible for server moderation settings?

Server owners and administrators are responsible for how Cadia is configured and used in their server.

This includes moderation tools, logging, tickets, automod, welcome messages, RPG settings, dashboard settings, and other server features.

## Can Cadia prevent all spam, raids, or rule-breaking?

No. Cadia can help with moderation and automation, but no bot can detect or prevent everything.

Server staff should still actively manage and moderate their communities.

## Can Cadia data be reset?

Yes. Cadia developers may reset, edit, remove, or restore data when needed because of bugs, abuse, cheating, technical issues, balancing, or updates.

## Where can I get support?

You can get support by joining the Cadia [Support Server](https://discord.gg/26R7kXa6dx).
